Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

-Do’s
Maintain a professional and respectful profile
Share informative and meaningful content
Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
Check privacy settings regularly
Think before posting anything online

-Don’ts
Do not share personal or sensitive information (OTP, address, etc.)
Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
Do not spread misinformation or unverified news
Avoid using weak or common passwords
Do not engage in arguments or cyberbullying online
